Milestones in the Journey Institute Growth

2008- Establishment of Government Polytechnic Awasari;with four Diploma Engg. Programs namely Automobile, Computer, Information Technology and E&TC.
2009- Addition of three new programs namely Civil Engg., Mechanical Engg., and Electrical Engg.
2010- Hostel for Girls with intake capacity 288 .
2011- The institute building is inaugurated by former Union Agricultural Minister Mr. Sharadchandraji Pawar on 15th January 2011.
2012- Hostel for Boys with intake capacity 336 started.
2012- Zonal level IEDSSA sports events for boys and girls organized.
2014- State level IEDSSA sports events for girls organized.
2016- Registration and inauguration of Alumni Association.
2017- Registration and Opening of ISTE and IEI student chapter.
2018- Opening of IE(I) Students Chapter
2019- Opening of ISTE Students Chapter

Our Vision And Mission

Vision- To inculcate Technical and Life skills among Rural masses to make them competent Technocrats and efficient Nation builders.

Mission- M1-To enrich with fundamental knowledge and state-of-art Technologies to meet the requirements of Industry and Society.
M2- To develop skills among Rural masses to become self-employable.
M3- To impart value based Education to serve society. M4. To create inquisitive attitude towards lifelong learning.
